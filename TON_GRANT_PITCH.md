# Skily (Sdadim DGT Prep) - TON Integration Pitch

## 1. Project Overview
**Name:** Skily (Sdadim DGT Prep)
**Vertical:** Social / EdTech / AI Mini App
**Stage:** Mainnet (Product is live, integrating TON ecosystem)
**One-liner:** AI-driven EdTech Telegram Mini App for driving exam preparation with gamification, duels, and TON-based rewards.

## 2. The Problem
Preparing for driving exams (like the DGT in Spain) is traditionally boring, non-interactive, and lacks community engagement. Existing solutions do not leverage the Telegram ecosystem or Web3 incentives to keep users motivated.

## 3. The Solution & TON Integration
Skily is a Progressive Web App (PWA) and Telegram Mini App that gamifies the learning process. It features:
- **AI Mentor (Skily):** Provides instant, localized feedback to students.
- **Multiplayer Duels:** Users compete in real-time knowledge battles.
- **TON Ecosystem (Why we integrate):**
  - **Smart Payments:** Seamless premium unlocking and booster purchases via TON/Telegram Stars.
  - **Web3 Onboarding:** Transitioning traditional EdTech users into Web3 through reward mechanics (XP to TON/Tokens).
  - **Wallet Connection:** Fully integrated `@tonconnect/ui-react` for instant secure login and transactions.

## 4. Market & User Base
- **Target Audience:** Driver's license candidates in Spain (and expanding to other regions).
- **Platform:** Natively built for Telegram UX, capturing the enormous mobile-first audience.

## 5. Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** React 18, Vite, Tailwind, Zustand, Telegram UI.
- **Backend:** Supabase (Database, Auth, Edge Functions for AI and Matchmaking).
- **Web3:** TON Connect SDK, TON Core.
