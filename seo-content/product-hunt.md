## Description (260 chars max)
Skilyapp is the smartest way to prepare for Spain's DGT driving theory test. AI adapts to your mistakes, 3000+ official questions, real exam simulation, PvP duels, road signs guide. Free. Works in Telegram too.

## Full Description
We built Skilyapp because passing the DGT theory exam in Spain is harder than it looks — especially for expats and immigrants who need to study in a language that isn't their first.

**The problem with existing tools:**
- Old apps dump 3000 questions on you with no structure
- No explanation of WHY an answer is correct
- No gamification → students quit after 2 days
- No support for English or Russian speakers

**What Skilyapp does differently:**

🧠 **AI Tutor 24/7** — Ask anything about Spanish traffic rules, get a clear explanation instantly. The AI also tracks your weak spots and adapts the test order accordingly.

🎮 **Gamified learning** — Real-time PvP duels against other learners, daily streaks, coin rewards. Learning feels like a game.

📋 **Real exam simulation** — Exact DGT format: 30 questions, 30 minutes, max 3 mistakes. Know if you're ready before you walk into the exam room.

🚦 **Road signs guide** — Interactive visual guide to all Spanish traffic signs with quizzes.

🌍 **3 languages** — Full support for Spanish, English, and Russian. Perfect for expats.

📱 **Works everywhere** — Web app + Telegram Mini App (@skilyapp_bot). Offline-capable.

**Results:** 9 out of 10 Skilyapp users pass on their first attempt.
